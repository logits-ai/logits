# NeuroSpeak: The Neuro-Inclusive Workspace

## Overview

**NeuroSpeak** is a specialized productivity workspace designed to adapt to the unique needs of neurodivergent minds. Built for individuals with ADHD, Autism, Dyslexia, and executive function challenges, it aims to lower the barrier to starting tasks by reducing digital noise and friction.

Unlike traditional to-do apps that can be overwhelming with complex menus and rigid structures, NeuroSpeak offers a fluid, sensory-friendly environment. It prioritizes "starting" over "organizing," helping users break through executive dysfunction and blank-page paralysis.
